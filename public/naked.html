Site Architecture

Internal Linking Strategy

Strategic internal links signal to search engines and AI which pages matter most.

Current Issue

Flat Link Distribution

Internal links are distributed evenly across all pages. Utility pages (Privacy Policy, Affiliates) receive similar link weight as flagship products. This prevents AI from identifying which products are most important for consumer queries.

Strategic Hierarchy

  • üèÜ
    Tier 1: Category Leaders Naked Whey, Naked Pea‚Äîmaximum internal links
  • üì¶
    Tier 2: Supporting Products Casein, Creatine, Collagen‚Äîmoderate links
  • üìÑ
    Tier 3: Utility Pages Minimal links from navigation only

Implementation Tactics

Contextual Product Links

Every blog post links to 2-3 relevant products within the content body

Hub-to-Product Connections

Knowledge hubs prominently link to related product pages

Cross-Sell Links

Product pages link to complementary products (Whey ‚Üí Creatine)

Anchor Text Optimization

Use descriptive anchors: "grass-fed whey protein" not "click here"
